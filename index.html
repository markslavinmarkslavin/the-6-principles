The 6 Principles

1DATA

What evidence already exists? Collect everything that doesn't contradict your goal.

2EXISTING

What frameworks already work? Build on existing solutions, don't reinvent.

3BOTTLENECK

What's the ONE thing that fails? Find what's missing, not what's difficult.

4CONCRETENESS

What exact condition must hold? Write the specific requirement, not the goal.

5CONTINUUM

How does it behave at extremes? Understand behavior at limits.

6SPECTRAL UNIFORMITY

What's the bridge to solution? The pattern that connects evidence to answer.

Where Can You Apply It?

Real problems. Real bottlenecks. Real solutions.

💼 Career

"Which job should I take?"

Bottleneck: What's your actual constraint — money, growth, location, or meaning?

📈 Business

"Why isn't my business growing?"

Bottleneck: Find the ONE metric that, if improved, cascades to everything else.

🩺 Health

"What's actually wrong with me?"

Bottleneck: What symptom, if resolved, would make other symptoms disappear?

💰 Investing

"Which investment makes sense?"

Bottleneck: What's the one assumption that, if wrong, changes everything?

🔧 Software

"Why does this bug exist?"

Bottleneck: Find the exact input that triggers failure. Everything else is noise.

⚖️ Legal

Bottleneck: What fact, if proven, decides the case? Focus there.

💔 Relationships

Bottleneck: What's the ONE unaddressed need driving all other conflicts?

🔬 Research

"Why isn't my experiment working?"
